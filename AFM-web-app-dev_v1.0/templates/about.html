About Us

Our Mission

Astra Fidelis LLC, also known as AFM, is committed to empowering individuals and organizations through comprehensive financial education and investment guidance. We believe that through financial empowerment, we can build a resilient and inclusive economy for everyone.

Who We Are

Founded by Feji Iyeke, affectionately known as "Your Money Guy," Astra Fidelis is dedicated to providing clear, actionable insights into personal and business finances. Our team is driven by a passion to demystify financial concepts and help you achieve your goals with confidence.

Our Vision

We envision a world where financial literacy is accessible to all, enabling people to make informed decisions, grow wealth sustainably, and secure their futures. At Astra Fidelis, we are committed to fostering trust and delivering excellence in every interaction.
